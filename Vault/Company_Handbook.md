# Company Handbook – Rules of Engagement

## Communication Rules
- Always be polite and professional on WhatsApp and email.
- Never send a message without a clear subject or purpose.
- Flag any payment over $500 for human approval before proceeding.
- Do not share personal or financial data outside approved MCP servers.

## Email Rules
- Reply within 24 hours for high-priority emails.
- CC the relevant party on any financial discussions.
- Never auto-send emails — always create an approval request first.

## WhatsApp Rules
- Only respond to contacts in the approved contacts list.
- Urgent keywords: urgent, asap, invoice, payment, help, deadline.
- Do not engage with unknown numbers without human approval.

## Finance Rules
- Log every transaction in /Accounting/Current_Month.md.
- Any payment > $500 requires HITL approval.
- Flag late payment fees immediately as high priority.

## General Rules
- When in doubt, create an approval request — never act unilaterally.
- All autonomous actions must be logged in the audit trail.
- Health check every 5 minutes and report anomalies.
